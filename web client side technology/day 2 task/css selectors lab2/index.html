About

Founded over two decades ago, the Information Technology Institute (ITI) provides world-classprofessional human professional human capacity building programs, hands-on training and an exposure to the latest technology trends for university students and graduates, academia and professionals, in collaboration with a wide network of partners from the ICT industry, the academia, and the community. Headquartered in Smart Village, and other five branches in Cairo, Alexandria, Mansoura, Assiut and Ismailia, ITI supplies local, regional and international markets with top notch trained professionals in the various IT fields. The close relation with local and international industry players helps ITI to provide around 82% of graduates with employment opportunities, four weeks prior to their graduation and around 95% within six weeks after their graduation. Through ITI talent accelerating model, several programs and initiatives are designed and implemented within three main pillars: Professional Development Skill Development Learning Outreach
